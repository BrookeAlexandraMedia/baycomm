Heavy Industry

Heavy industry deals with large products and high levels of capital investment, and often involves complex manufacturing processes.

It uses the products which are commonly used like oil, coal, ships, and iron. As the leading industrial products manufacturer, Nordan Industries specilizes in providing its customers with large and heavy products and equipment for the industry.
