Experience since 1999

Nordan Industries is the leading provider of specialty services to North America's process industries.

Our company was founded in 1999 as a local enterprise manufacturing various products and providing services for other industrial companies. We focus on long-term and sustainable development concepts.

Our Mission

We provide the highest-quality end products to our customers, while striving to make them the leaders in their respective industries.
